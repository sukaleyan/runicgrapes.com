wine-stained stories

Not all stains fade. Some deepen, seeping into memory, legend, and art. Here, wine drifts through centuries—a shadow in the background, a gleam in the foreground—leaving traces on frescoes, carvings, and canvases. These are tales where the vine and the imagination entwine, ripening into images that speak long after the last drop is gone.
